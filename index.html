The Power of Medicinal Mushrooms

Lion's Mane (Hericium erinaceus)

Known for its brain-boosting properties, Lion's Mane is a powerful nootropic. It contains compounds like hericenones and erinacines, which are believed to stimulate the growth of brain cells and protect against cognitive decline.

  • Supports cognitive function and memory
  • Promotes nerve growth factor (NGF) production
  • May reduce symptoms of anxiety and depression
  • Aids in digestive health

Turkey Tail (Trametes versicolor)

Turkey Tail is celebrated for its immune-modulating effects. Rich in polysaccharopeptides (PSPs) and polysaccharide K (PSK), it has been extensively studied for its potential to support the immune system, especially in conjunction with conventional treatments.

  • Boosts immune system function
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Supports gut health
  • May enhance the effectiveness of certain treatments
